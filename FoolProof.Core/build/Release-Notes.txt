- Many new *compare* validation attributes 
- New *predicate* validation attributes (`AndAttribute`, `OrAttribute`, `NotAttribute` and `IsValidAttribute`) to support complex validation rules through boolean predicates.
- Drop support for *MS Ajax Validation*.
- *jQuery* validation support for all the new validators, including the *predicate* ones.
- Support for complex model-wise validation rules (using *predicate* validators and `IsValidAttribute`).
